Second Brain Manifesto

Jul 2020
By Ruben Serradas
Category: secondbrain-manifesto
Tags: copywrite

Did you know our brain is loaded daily with 34 GB of information?

That's on average 11.8 hours of information per day. Almost 4 hours more than what we use to sleep or work.

That's why we think modern life can be tough.

How can we become the best version of ourselves if we're constantly bombarded with:

  • Information.
  • Decisions to take.
  • Things to do.

Introducing Second Brainio

We know things can become hectic sometimes.

Wouldn't it be nice if we had a second brain to save the things that we need to do later?

That's what we're building!

How does it work?

Second Brainio is a personal management tool, which will help you keep things simple:

  • Convention Over Configuration : We'll fix the chaos. Not only provide you with solutions.
  • Temporary : Choose what really matters to you.
  • Simple : We won't reinvent the wheel. We'll just help you use it properly.
